 Operator precedence In C

    3*x – 8y .  is (3x)-(8y) or.     3(2-84) ?
    In C language simple mathematical rules bike BODMAS, no longer applies.
    The answer to the above question is provided by by operator precedence & associativity
    Operator precedence: The following table lists the operator priority in C
           
    Priority           Operators
    1.	               *,    /,.  %
    2                  +,   -
    3                  =
    
    Operators of in the higher priority are evaluated first the absence of parenthesis.
    Operator Associativity: When operators of equal priorily are present present in an expression, the tie is taken care of by associativity.
    X*y/z.       -->     (xy) / z
    2/ y + z .   -->     (x/y) * 2
    *, /  follows Left to right associativity
